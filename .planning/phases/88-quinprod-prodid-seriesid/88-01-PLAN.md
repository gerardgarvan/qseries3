   - Product form: (-z;q)_∞ (-q/z;q)_∞ (z²q;q²)_∞ (q/z²;q²)_∞ (q;q)_∞
   - Use exact notation (ASCII subscripts OK; ∞ is Unicode U+221E).

4. Do NOT evaluate ev(0) or ev(1) for prodid/seriesid — the identity is symbolic and does not depend on z or q values. This allows quinprod(z,q,prodid) to work even when z and q are undefined.
5. Otherwise fall through to existing logic: return quinprod(ev(0), ev(1), static_cast<int>(evi(2))).
